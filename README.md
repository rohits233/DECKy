## Features

- **Document to deck** — Upload PDFs, Word docs, or paste raw text
- **Video to deck** — Drop in a client interview recording; Decky extracts insights and builds the narrative
- **One-click .pptx export** — Fully editable PowerPoint output, no formatting required
- **Integrated workflow** — Interview in, finished deck out, no manual steps in between
- **Stripe billing** — Subscription management with customer portal
- **Generation history** — Every deck saved and accessible from your dashboard
- **Secure auth** — User accounts with session management
